# Chrome Web Store Listing

## Name
MultiView — Multi-Video Viewer

## Short Description (132 chars max)
Watch up to 32 videos at once. Add videos from any site, then tile them across your screen in separate windows.

## Detailed Description

MultiView lets you watch multiple videos simultaneously by tiling browser windows across your screen. Works with YouTube, Twitch, and any video site.

HOW IT WORKS

1. Browse YouTube, Twitch, or any video site
2. Right-click a video link and select "Add to MultiView"
3. Click the MultiView icon and hit "Tile Windows"
4. All your videos open in separate windows, perfectly arranged in a grid

FEATURES

- Watch up to 32 videos at the same time
- Works on ANY website — YouTube, Twitch, sports streams, and more
- Tile Windows arranges videos in a grid across your screen
- Twitch streams open in theater mode for minimal clutter
- YouTube videos autoplay when tiled
- Toast notifications confirm when videos are added
- Queue manager to view and organize your video list
- Paste URLs directly to add videos
- Close all tiled windows with one click from any window
- Floating MultiView controls on every tiled window
- Keyboard shortcut Alt+M to open the queue manager

PERFECT FOR

- Sports fans watching multiple games
- Twitch viewers following several streams
- Monitoring multiple live feeds
- Comparing video content side by side

NO ADS. NO TRACKING. Your video queue is stored locally on your device and never leaves your browser.
